Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

The English version of this Privacy Policy is the legally binding version. The Polish translation is provided for informational purposes only.

1. Who We Are

MyMedStock is developed and operated by MBJ Studio.

Email: support@mymedstock.app

2. What MyMedStock Is (and Is Not)

MyMedStock is a personal medication inventory management app. It helps you track your medication stock levels and estimate how long your supply will last.

MyMedStock is not:

  • a medical app,
  • a health records system,
  • a prescription management system,
  • a clinical decision support tool.

All medical decisions remain solely with you and your healthcare provider.

3. Who Can Use MyMedStock

MyMedStock is intended for users aged 18 and older. By using the app, you confirm that you are at least 18 years of age.

4. What Data We Collect

4.1 Account Data

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (stored in encrypted form — we never see your actual password)

If you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and display name from Google.

Please note that your email address may indirectly reveal personal information, such as your name or your employer, depending on the format of the address (e.g. firstname.lastname@employer.com). We treat your email address with the same care as other personal data and use it solely for authentication and account management purposes.

4.2 Medication Data

Data you enter manually:

  • Medication names
  • Medication forms (tablet, capsule, gel, etc.)
  • Dosage information
  • Stock quantities
  • Expiry dates

This data is stored in your personal account and is not shared with anyone.

Important: Medication information you enter may indirectly reveal information about your health conditions. We treat this data with the same care as health-related data. It is processed solely to provide app functionality, is never used for advertising, is never sold to third parties, and is never used for profiling.

4.3 Technical Data

We may collect basic technical information to ensure the app works correctly:

  • App version
  • Device operating system version
  • Error logs (in case of app crashes)

We do not collect: location data, device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID), or usage analytics beyond what is necessary for app functionality.

We do not use cookies. Some preferences (such as notification settings and app theme) are stored locally on your device using standard Android storage mechanisms and are never transmitted to our servers.

5. How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to:

  • provide the core functionality of the app (medication tracking, stock calculations),
  • perform supply estimates and planning calculations based exclusively on data you provide,
  • send you push notifications you have configured (weekly summaries, low stock alerts),
  • maintain and improve the app.

We do not: sell your data to third parties, use your data for advertising, share your data with pharmaceutical companies or healthcare providers, or use your medication data for any purpose other than displaying it to you.

6. Supply Calculations

All stock calculations, appointment planning estimates, and trip planning results are estimates based entirely on data you provide. Calculations may become inaccurate if you enter incorrect data, forget to update your stock, change your dosage without recording it, lose medication, or consume medication differently than recorded.

MyMedStock does not guarantee the correctness, completeness, or suitability of any calculation. You remain responsible for independently verifying medication quantities and expiry dates with the physical packaging before making any decisions.

7. Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored on servers provided by Supabase (Supabase Inc.), located in the European Union (Ireland — AWS eu-west-1 region). Supabase acts as our data processor. Their privacy policy is available at: supabase.com/privacy

Data storage within the EU means your data is protected under GDPR.

8. How Long We Keep Your Data

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all your personal data is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days. This action is irreversible.

You can delete your account at any time from: Settings → Delete account and data.

9. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a resident of the European Union, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data directly in the app.
  • Right to erasure — delete your account and all associated data at any time.
  • Right to data portability — request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object — object to the processing of your data.
  • Right to restriction of processing — request that we limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw consent at any time, where applicable.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: support@mymedstock.app

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority. In Poland, this is the Urząd Ochrony Danych Osobowych (UODO): uodo.gov.pl

10. Push Notifications

If you enable push notifications, we will send you:

  • Weekly medication stock summaries (on a day and time you choose in Settings),
  • Critical low stock alerts (when a medication falls below your configured threshold),
  • Prescription pickup reminders (for medications marked as awaiting collection).

Push notifications are a convenience feature. MyMedStock does not guarantee that notifications will be delivered. Notification delivery depends on your device settings, operating system, battery optimisation, and network connectivity. You remain solely responsible for managing your medication supply — do not rely exclusively on notifications to track your medications.

You can disable notifications at any time in App Settings → Notifications, or your device's system notification settings.

12. Security

We take the security of your data seriously: all data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), passwords are never stored in plain text, and access to the database is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) — you can only access your own data.

However, no system is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please contact us immediately at: support@mymedstock.app

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app. Minor updates (such as clarifications or corrections) may be made without notice. The date of the last update is always shown at the top of this document.

Continued use of the app after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
